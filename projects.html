Projectile Launcher Rework

This is a mod I created called Projectile Launcher Rework. It is a mod for the video game Cyberpunk 2077 and is now in the 2.0 phase. With over 30k downloads, a lot of people have shown that they appreciate and like the work that I do with this. The purpose of this mod is to redo the launcher weapons in the game. I added new launchers, new rounds, and some new tweaks to how launchers work.
